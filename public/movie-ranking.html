Jessi's Favorite Movies!!!

My top 3 (meaningful) movies of all-time.

The Matrix

I saw this movie in 1999.
The plot had an impact on me so much that I decided to become vegetarian.

The Matrix: Reloaded

I saw this movie in May of 2003.
The next month I finalized my divorce.

The Matix: Revolutions

I saw this movie in November of 2003.
At this time I was in a new wonderful and loving relationship.
